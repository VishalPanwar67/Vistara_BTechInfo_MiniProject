Admission Process

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the basic requirements:

  • Completion of 12th grade
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM)

Step 2: Entrance Exams

Clear the necessary entrance exams:

  • National Level: JEE Main, JEE Advanced
  • State Level: Refer to state-specific exams

Step 3: Counselling and Seat Allotment

Participate in counselling sessions after exam results:

  • Choose colleges and courses based on your rank

Computer Science

Course Details: The course deals with the development and designing of computer software and computer technologies. It provides training and knowledge in algorithms, programming languages, and program design. The course involves foundations in mathematics and engineering and incorporates techniques from queueing theory, probability and statistics, and electronic circuit design.
Fees: The fees for the entire duration range from Rs. 2 Lakhs to Rs. 7.20 Lakhs1 or even up to Rs. 15 lakhs.

Job Profiles: Graduates can opt for various careers, such as Computer Hardware Engineer, Software Developer, Database Administrator, Web Developer.

Mechanical Engineering

Course Details: The course deals with the principles of mechanical engineering for designing, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. The course involves foundations in mathematics and engineering and incorporates techniques from queueing theory, probability and statistics, and electronic circuit design.
Fees: The fees for the entire duration range from INR 50,000 to INR 7,00,000 in public institutes and between INR 3,00,000 to INR 15,00,000 in private engineering colleges.
Average Salary: The average salary after completing B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering is around INR 4,00,000 to 6,00,000.
Job Profiles: Graduates can opt for various careers, such as Mechanical Engineers, Service Engineer, Consultant, Instructional Designer, Mechanical Designer etc.
Top Recruiters: Some of the top recruiters that hire Mechanical Engineering graduates are BMW, General Electric Co (GE), General Motors Corporation, Ford Motor Company, Caterpillar, Inc., Larsen and Toubro Ltd, etc.
